Privacy Policy for QvA Tuner

Last updated: 2025/02/12

1. App Functionality
This app analyzes the audio stream incoming through your device's microphone to display the current frequency in real-time. 

2. Data Usage
- The sound stream is analyzed instantly and then discarded
- No audio recording or storage takes place
- No audio data is transmitted anywhere

3. Analytics
The app uses standard Google Analytics to collect basic usage statistics to help improve app performance and user experience.

4. Data Collection
Beyond standard Google Analytics, no personal data or audio information is collected, stored, or shared with any third parties.
